Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the information you submit through this intake form. This form is operated by the cleaning business you are contacting, using Kardama's platform.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Service address and home details
  • Scheduling preferences and service frequency
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your cleaning service request
  • To schedule and confirm appointments
  • To send SMS and email communications related to your request
  • To improve the quality of our services

SMS Communications

By submitting this form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from the cleaning business regarding your request, scheduling updates, and service confirmations. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP to any SMS message or contact us directly via email.

Data Sharing

Your information is shared only with the cleaning business you contacted and Kardama, the technology platform powering this form. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the requested services and comply with applicable laws. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the business directly.

Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage, which maintains SOC 2 compliance.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the cleaning business you submitted this form to, or reach Kardama at hello@kardama.ai.
