Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 2, 2026  ·  Powered by Kardama

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data Kardama holds about you. This page explains how to make that request and what to expect.

What Data We May Hold

  • Information you submitted via an intake form (name, email, phone, address, scheduling preferences)
  • Messages exchanged through SMS, Facebook Messenger, or Instagram Direct Messages with a Kardama-powered business
  • Facebook or Instagram identifiers (PSID, IGSID) and public profile name received when you message a connected business
  • Service history with the cleaning business you contacted

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to kardama.ai@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. In the body, include the email address, phone number, or Facebook/Instagram handle the data is associated with, plus a short note confirming you are the owner of that account

Quick link: Send a pre-filled deletion request

What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 7 days
  • We will delete your personal data within 30 days of verifying your identity
  • This includes any data received from Facebook or Instagram (messages, profile name, page-scoped IDs)
  • Some records may be retained where required by law (for example, payment records for tax purposes), and will be limited to what is legally required

Facebook and Instagram Users

If you contacted a Kardama-powered business through Facebook Messenger or Instagram Direct Messages, you can also remove the conversation from Meta's side at any time:

  • Messenger: open the conversation → tap the name at the top → Block or Delete chat
  • Instagram: open the conversation → tap the name → Block, or long-press the chat in your inbox and choose Delete

To remove the data we hold on our servers, please email us using the steps above.

Contact

Questions about deletion or your data? Email kardama.ai@gmail.com.
